WHEREAS the Owner is the absolute owner of the property situated at Flat No. 302, 3rd Floor, Green Valley Apartments, Indiranagar, Bangalore as detailed in Annexure-I, hereinafter referred to as "Demised Premises".

WHEREAS the Tenant has requested the Owner to grant Rent with respect to the Schedule Premises and the Owner has agreed to rent out to the Tenant the Property with two-wheeler and four-wheeler parking space in the ground floor for residential purposes only, on the following terms and conditions:
NOW THIS DEED WITNESSETH AS FOLLOWS:

    Term: The rent in respect of the “Demised Premises” shall commence from 1st February 2026 and shall be valid till 31st December 2026 (11 Months). Thereafter, the same may be extended further on mutual consent of both the parties.

    Rent & Late Payment Penalty: That the Tenant shall pay to the Owner a monthly rent of Rs. 25,000/- (Rupees Twenty-Five Thousand Only), excluding electricity and water bill. The rent shall be paid on or before the 7th day of each month without fail.

        Penalty Clause: In the event of a delay in payment of rent beyond the 7th of the month, the Tenant shall pay a penalty of Rs. 500/- per day for every day of delay until the rent is paid in full.

    Maintenance: That the Tenant shall pay to the Owner a monthly maintenance charge of Rs. 3,000/- towards the maintenance of Generator & Elevator, Salaries towards guards, Charges for Electricity Maintenance for Common Areas, Charges towards cleaning of Common Areas and towards maintaining the lawn.

    Utilities (Fixed): That the Tenant shall pay for the running cost of elevator and generator separately to the Owner (if applicable/not covered in maintenance).

    Utilities (Variable): That during the Rent period, in addition to the rental amount payable to the Owner, the Tenant shall pay for the use of electricity and water as per bills received from the authorities concerned directly.

    Security Deposit: The Tenant will pay to the Owner an interest-free refundable security deposit of Rs. 1,50,000/- (Rupees One Lakh Fifty Thousand Only) vide cheque no 456123 dated 20th January 2026 at the time of signing the Rent Agreement.

        The said amount shall be refunded by the Owner to the Tenant at the time of handing over possession upon expiry or termination after adjusting any dues or cost towards damages.

        Penalty Clause: If the Owner fails to refund the security deposit on valid termination/expiry, the Tenant is entitled to hold possession without payment of rent until the refund is made.

    Fittings: That all the sanitary, electrical and other fittings and fixtures and appliances in the premises shall be handed over from the Owner to the Tenant in good working condition.

    Usage: That the Tenant shall not sublet, assign or part with the demised premises in whole or part thereof to any person in any circumstances whatsoever and the same shall be used for bonafide residential purposes only.

    Repairs: That the day-to-day minor repairs (up to Rs. 1,000/- per instance) will be the responsibility for the Tenant at his/her own expense. Structural or major repairs shall be carried out by the Owner.

    Alterations: That no structural additions or alterations shall be made by the Tenant without the prior written consent of the Owner.

    Inspection: That the Owner shall hold the right to visit in person or through his authorized agent(s) to enter upon the demised premises for inspection (not exceeding once in a month) with prior notice.

    Compliance: That the Tenant shall comply with all the rules and regulations of the local authority and the Apartment Association.

    Taxes: That the Owner shall pay for all taxes/cesses (Property Tax) levied on the premises.

    Quiet Possession: That the Owner will keep the Tenant free and harmless from any claims or actions by others with respect to quiet possession of the premises.

    Termination & Lock-in Period:

        This Rent Agreement can be terminated before the expiry of this tenancy period by serving One Month prior notice in writing by either party.

        Lock-in Penalty: However, there is a mandatory Lock-in Period of 6 (Six) Months. If the Tenant vacates the premises before the completion of 6 months, the Tenant shall be liable to pay the rent for the remaining months of the lock-in period or forfeit the equivalent amount from the Security Deposit.

    Handover: The Tenant shall maintain the Demised Premises in good condition. On expiry or termination, the Tenant must hand over the premises in the same condition subject to natural wear and tear.

    Overstay Penalty: That in case where the Premises are not vacated by the Tenant at the termination of the Rent period, the Tenant will pay damages calculated at two times (2x) the monthly rent for any period of occupation commencing from the expiry of the Rent period.

    Cheque Bounce Penalty: In the event that any cheque issued by the Tenant towards Rent or Security Deposit is dishonored/bounced by the bank for any reason, the Tenant shall be liable to pay a flat penalty of Rs. 1,500/- per bounced cheque, in addition to the actual bank charges incurred by the Owner.

    Painting Charges: Upon vacating the premises, a sum of Rs. 15,000/- (Rupees Fifteen Thousand) or the actual cost of painting (whichever is higher) shall be deducted from the Security Deposit towards fresh painting of the premises.

    Dispute Resolution: In case of any dispute to this agreement and the clauses herein, the same will be settled in the jurisdiction of the Bangalore civil courts.

    Registration: That the Rent Agreement will be registered, and charges towards stamp duty, court fee & lawyer/coordinator will be equally borne by the Owner and Tenant.

Extraordinary Events & Risk Allocation:

That notwithstanding anything contained herein, it is expressly agreed as follows:

That in the event the Demised Premises becomes partially or wholly unusable due to reasons including but not limited to government or municipal orders, sealing by statutory authorities, changes in applicable laws or zoning regulations, failure of essential infrastructure or services, decisions or actions of the Apartment Association or neighboring occupants, pandemic-related restrictions, or any other circumstance beyond the direct control of the Owner, the Tenant shall not be entitled to any reduction, suspension, waiver, or refund of rent or maintenance charges.

That during the continuance of such events, the Tenant shall continue to pay the full monthly rent, maintenance, and all other charges payable under this Agreement, without demur or delay.

That the Tenant shall not claim early termination, compensation, damages, or alternative accommodation costs from the Owner on account of such events.

That the Owner shall not be liable for any loss, hardship, inconvenience, or expenses suffered by the Tenant arising out of or in connection with such extraordinary events.
